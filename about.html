Our Story

As the world grappled with the twin health and economic crisis caused by the Covid-19 Pandemic and the loss of employment by the vast majority, as a result of retrenchment, create the very genesis concept of Lisab & Co. Basil Prince Paynter and Immediate family members, decided to established the company called Lisab & Co. The main purpose for the establishment of Lisab & Co. was to contribute to personal, social and economic development of the country and advance the sustainable of business venture. Basil was mindful of the well-established competitors within the Management System Certification and Accreditation Training Sector. For Lisab and Company (Pty) Ltd to gain much needed traction, we firmly believe that we need to offer carefully selected Management System Certification and Accreditation Training Services that touch the hearts of our clients in a profound manner, at an economically cost, making Lisab and Company (Pty) Ltd the trusted brand at all times.

  • Mission: To Simplified Management System Certification and Accreditation Training processes for sustainable economic growth
  • Vision: To become the ultimate choice of service provided in the Certification and Training Sector
  • Accreditation Registration: Lisab and Company is in the processes of SITA/ QCTO Registration
